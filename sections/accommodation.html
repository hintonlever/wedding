Accommodation

Keppel Island is beautiful and quiet.

We recommend staying at the Great Keppel Island Hideaway where the wedding will be held. A variety of rooms have been reserved for everyone on the Fri and Sat nights at a negotiated rate.

You are welcome to extend before or after the 2 nights. Note that there are limited room types available on the Sunday night.

Rooms can be secured on a first come first serve basis. Please contact the Hideaway directly and mention Alina & Ben's group booking.
