Travel Info

Getting There

Fly to Rockhampton, then drive to Rosslyn Bay Marina (45 mins). Ferries depart regularly to Great Keppel Island - we'll coordinate group bookings!

Closest airport is Rockhampton (ROK), with multiple flights per day from Brisbane.

From Rockampton airport, it's a 50-minute drive to the ferry. There are limited ubers, but there are taxis. We recommend carpooling where possible.

Ferries depart from Rosslyn Bay Marina in Yeppoon. Either $55pp return on Keppel Connections or $60pp return on Freedom Fast Cats. Make sure you go to the right pier, it is not walkable between.

Once on the island everything is walkable
